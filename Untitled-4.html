Jobs and Oppotunites

So what are some jobs in cybercrime?

There are a number of jobs that have originated as a result of the internet 

Some jobs include...

  • White Hat Hacker

    • Proffesional hackers that are hired by companies to build, test and fix firewalls and protection services.
    Salary: $100,000

    These people are esentially hackers, except they use their hacking ability to better protect the internet. This profession began when the need for testing website security and protecting data on the internet became even more essential since more Black Hat Hackers have attempted to steal data, thus created a need for people who knew how to create highly effective protection systems against data-breaches.

  • Cybercrime Researcher

    • Further develop and improve security services in your corperation and find new potential threats towards security systems.
    Salary: $110,000

    This profession is quite similar to white hat hackers and Cyber Security analayst, except these people mainly manage and further investigate new potential security threats. The need of these type of workers came right after hacking the security
